You need to meet the YouTube monetization requirements, to monetize your channel on YouTube. Everyone knows, Google AdSense is the primary source to make money on YouTube. But you need to pass these barriers.

However, don't worry it's not much complicated than you think. So, in this post, let me explain the YouTube monetization requirements with other questions running on your mind and also a few more tips to achieve it.

Requirements:

  • You need atleast 1,000 subscribers
  • Have 4,000+ watch hours in the last 12 months
  • Follow all the YouTube monetization policies
  • Live in a country where YPP is available
  • Have a linked AdSense account
  • Get reviewed

How long does it take to achieve monetization requirements?

It depends on how you’re dealing with your channel. Some peoples achieve it within 30 days, and for few, it takes a year. On average it would take around 5 to 6 months of a good time.

Copyright and Monetization Policies on YouTube

Among YouTube's policies and guidelines is the huge topic of copyrighted material. Essentially, if you use other people's work, then you could be using copyrighted material without their permission, and YouTube does not want you to do that. It will almost certainly get you demonetized or prevent you from entering the YouTube Partner Program.

Important Points To Consider:

Use Keywords: Keywords are the heart of videos to get more views. So, use the proper keywords in title, description, and tags.
Analyze competitor: Lookout what kind of videos your competitor making. Analyze their thumbnails, video quality, keywords used by them, and many more.
Make Lengthy Videos: To achieve the watch hours quickly, try to make the useful lengthy videos. Moreover, my suggestion is to take 10-15 minutes of length for a video.
Consistent and Persistent: Publish few videos, then withdraw for a month won’t give anything. Concentrate the next 6 months for your channel consistently.
Don’t Steal: Kindly don’t steal the other videos and put them on your channel. It may cause to override the YouTube guidelines.
Add Custom Thumbnails: Create and add custom thumbnails. Thumbnails help to get more clicks to the videos. And on each video, add the watermark subscribe button. It helps to get more subscribers in a short period.
Instead of concentrating on making money, try to provide valuable content to your audience. In a nutshell, once the audience welcomes your video, then the YouTube monetization requirement is achievable and it leads to making money.
