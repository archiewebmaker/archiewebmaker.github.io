The Right SEO Approach

Hummingbird is the name of the update that introduced semantic search, Google's way of deciphering user intent rather than mapping out individual keywords and phrases.

Today, Google attempts to understand meaning rather than matching keywords, so keyword-centric optimization doesn't work the same way. However, keyword research is still relevant, as it can help guide your strategic focus and provide you with ranking opportunities.

The right SEO approach is to tell the search engine what's your site all about in all clearity and honesty. Don't try to manipulate or game the system. You will only get penalized which is bad for your business.
