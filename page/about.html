A Humble beginning of Archie Webmaker

I created my first "Hello World" page in 1998 using Microsoft Frontpage in Trivandrum while helping out a friend on his thesis.

I was amazed and hooked by the gibberish looking code that converts into a beautiful design!

True to the core, this was what I exactly wanted in my life.

After I built my first website in 2000 on Geocities and getting instant attention from others, I decided to pursue a career in this newfound passion. And the part-time freelancing journey of 'Archie Webmaker' began.

On April 1, 2002, I was awarded a Diploma in Information Technology from Sikkim Manipal University.

While in New Delhi in 2004, I got introduced to blogging and eventually published my first blog called "www.greports.com" - a blog about Northeast India's current affair.

In 2008, I was dub as the most talkative user (weblogian) by Blogcatalog, a US-based blogger community, and eventually, one of my free WordPress themes became a star.

In 2011, I got the opportunity to design the first-ever website of Trinity Theological College, and that's when I decided to turn it into a full business.

Since then, I've been providing the resources and services to educational institutes, professionals, and businesses. I want to share my experiences, and I have a message for the world, "Success begins when you truly fall in love with yourself."

I come to work every day because I want to solve problems and make the internet more beautiful. We live in a world of too much information. Too much advice and too little insight, and so I candidly shepherd aspiring entrepreneurs through business ideas, building brand, marketing, and everything else that matters in this blog.
