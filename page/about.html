Perhaps, it is time for you to know about my story.

They told me I couldn't.

No one believed in my dreams, including my family and my friends.

I had no money, no income to start any of my dreams.

I spent 20 years equipping myself and freelancing at the same time.

I then asked myself, "why am I not successful despite being talented and hardworking?"

A wise man once said, "Happiness is not in the mere possession of money; it lies in the joy of achievement, in the thrill of creative effort."

I kept doing until I reach my target and goal.

Luck nor opportunity knocked at me, so I built a door.

Today, with a smile on my face, I tell myself every day, I've achieved my success because they told me I couldn't.

Yes, they told me I couldn't, and that's why I did.
