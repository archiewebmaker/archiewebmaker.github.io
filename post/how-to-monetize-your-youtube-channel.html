Copyright and Monetization Policies on YouTube

Among YouTube's policies and guidelines is the huge topic of copyrighted material. Essentially, if you use other people's work, then you could be using copyrighted material without their permission, and YouTube does not want you to do that. It will almost certainly get you demonetized or prevent you from entering the YouTube Partner Program.

Important Points To Consider:

Use Keywords: Keywords are the heart of videos to get more views. So, use the proper keywords in title, description, and tags.
Analyze competitor: Lookout what kind of videos your competitor making. Analyze their thumbnails, video quality, keywords used by them, and many more.
Make Lengthy Videos: To achieve the watch hours quickly, try to make the useful lengthy videos. Moreover, my suggestion is to take 10-15 minutes of length for a video.
Consistent and Persistent: Publish few videos, then withdraw for a month won’t give anything. Concentrate the next 6 months for your channel consistently.
Don’t Steal: Kindly don’t steal the other videos and put them on your channel. It may cause to override the YouTube guidelines.
