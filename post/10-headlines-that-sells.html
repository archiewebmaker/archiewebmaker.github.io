10 Stunning Headlines That Sells

Published on 04-08-20205 min readMarketing

When it comes to selling a product, whether it is for yourself or for your client, nothing is more important than the quality of the product itself. If you have a good product, pitching it and attracting word-of-mouth advertising will be easy. Customers will recognize that your product has intrinsic value; and they will purchase it and spread the word to their friends.

When selling that same product, the next most important thing is the quality of your sales pitch. Even if your product is excellent, you won’t get a lot of word-ofmouth advertising unless you have a few brave souls who are willing to purchase the product initially to kick things off. The only way that will happen is if you persuade them to. And that’s where your copywritingskills comes in.

Copywriting is the art of using sales copy to persuade readers to become buyers.

Crafting a Winning Headline

A winning headline has five important components.

  • Speak to Your Audience
  • Use Psychological Trigger Words
  • Read News Headlines for Inspiration
  • Use a Good Headline Formula
  • State a Major Benefit or Propose a Puzzling Question

We will consider some "fill-in-the-blanks" headlines. All you have to do is pick a headline from the correct category,fill in the blanks, and add it to the top of your newsletter or squeeze page. Shape the rest of your copy around your chosen headline.

01. Are You Ready to Get Serious About [Job/Product/Task]?

02. Are You Prepared to Take Your Future into Your Own Hands?

03. Learn the Top Five Things that Science Suggests YouShould Do to Become Successful at [Career/Business].

04. Use My Tested Methods for [Achieving Goal #1] and [Achieving Goal #2] in Just Days!

05. Change Your Life Overnight with [Name of Your Product]!

06. Learn How to Change Your Life and Your Career in Just [# of Days] Days Using My Proven System.

07. Learn How to Triple The Amount of Traffic That Comes to Your Site in Just [# of Days] Days.

08. Learn How to Transform Your [Career/Hobby/Relationship] in Just [# of Days] Days and [# of Steps] Steps!

09. How to Build an Empire from the Ashes of Your Failed Business Venture

10. Seven Explosive Secrets that are Guaranteed to [Product’s Promise]

Writing good copy is never an easy task. And perhaps hardest of all is writing a good headline. Striking the right balance between enticing the reader with language and hooking them with a benefit or a question can often be difficult. Fortunately for you, this guide takes much of the hard work out of the process.

From here, you determine your own path. You might start by using the advice I’ve given for writing headlines. Craft a few of your own, think about how you would react to them, and ask a friend, neighbor, orfellow marketer to evaluate the quality of your copy.

Once you have gotten the hang of writing headlines in general, think harder about your niche in particular. Do market research if needed; and think long and hard about what the average buyer thinks like—and what that average buyer wants from a product like yours.

After you have answered these questions, grab a headline from one of the templates above; and then tailor it to your particular needs. Start by filling in the blanks, but if needed, go further and make alterations to the rest of the headline.
